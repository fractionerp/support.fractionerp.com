# Master Production Schedule (MPS)

Details for Master Production Schedule (MPS) under Production.

A Master Production Schedule (MPS) is a key tool in any factory to understand the rate of production (or delivery schedule). In Fraction ERP we can display the MPS in terms of quantity or money.
